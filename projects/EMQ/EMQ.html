EMQ Online Retail Store


This online website was a team project following the Agile methodology that had several requirements.


Problem statement – New local electronic retailer chain, “EMQ”, in the Bay Area counties (San Francisco, San Mateo, Santa Clara, Alameda, Contra Costa) wants to add delivery services as part of their business plan. First, use population density information and map to determine strategically where to open their 2 stores per county. Second, make sure each store is optimally located so deliveries can be speedy. You are responsible to develop the website where customer can purchase electronic devices (e.g. TVs, stereos, computers, …, etc. into a virtual shopping cart. Pay for it online and have it delivery to their home. Need to be able to track the delivery truck via Google map. Inventory, customer, transactions are processed and stored in RDBMS.


In Simpler Terms:

  • Must be full-stack with languages of your choice. (PHP/HTML/MySQL)
  • Locally hosted. (We used MAMP, which bundles Apache and PHP)
  • Must have a "cart" or a returning session.
  • Determine locations for optimal delivery..
  • Must simulate a full online transaction.
  • Must simulate sample delivery and item tracking.
  • Must work on mobile devices as well.
